The Problem

Most founders build HR the same way.

They hire fast when things are going well. They write policies when something goes wrong. They bring in an HR consultant after a key person leaves and the team starts asking questions nobody has answers to.

By that point the damage is already done. Not irreparably. But expensively.

The companies that scale without culture collapse are not the ones with the best product or the most funding. They are the ones that built people systems before they needed them rather than after.

That is what PeopleFirst does. We help you build the structure that lets your people do their best work, before the moment you desperately need it.

Service Callouts

HR infrastructure from scratch. If you are past 10 employees and still running on gut feel and WhatsApp groups, you have a people problem waiting to happen. We audit what you have, identify the gaps, and build the policies, processes, and documentation your company needs to grow without breaking.

Hiring systems that find the right people. A bad hire at a 50-person company costs more than money. It costs time, morale, and the trust of the team watching how you handle it. We build hiring frameworks that filter for culture and competence before the offer letter goes out.

Retention strategies that actually work. According to Gallup's 2024 State of the Global Workplace report, 59 percent of employees worldwide are quietly quitting. They are still showing up. They have just stopped caring. We help you identify why people disengage and build the systems that keep your best people invested.

About Page

PeopleFirst was built on one observation.

Most growing companies do not have an HR problem. They have a systems problem that shows up in their people.

High turnover. Low engagement. Managers who were great individual contributors and are now struggling to lead. Founders who care deeply about culture but have never had the tools to build it intentionally.

These are not character failures. They are infrastructure gaps. And infrastructure gaps have solutions.

We are a team of HR strategists and organisational design consultants who have spent the last decade working inside fast-growing companies across Africa and beyond. We have seen what happens when people systems are built reactively. We have also seen what becomes possible when they are built with intention.

PeopleFirst exists to give growing companies the second version of that story.

Services Page

HR Audit and Infrastructure Design. We start by understanding exactly where you are. What policies exist. What processes are followed. What is documented and what lives only in someone's head. From there we design the infrastructure your company needs to grow to the next stage without the people problems that derail most scaling companies.

Hiring Framework Development. We build end-to-end hiring systems including job design, interview frameworks, assessment tools, and offer processes that are consistent, defensible, and designed to find the right person not just the most available one.

Manager Development. Your managers determine your retention more than your salary packages. Research from Gallup consistently shows that 70 percent of the variance in team engagement is explained by the manager. We build practical development programmes that turn your best individual contributors into leaders their teams actually want to work for.

Culture and Engagement Strategy. Culture is not a values statement on a wall. It is the sum of every decision made when nobody is watching. We help you understand what your culture actually is, not what you want it to be, and build the systems that close the gap between the two.

Conflict Resolution and Policy Development. When something goes wrong with a person, the cost of handling it badly is always higher than the cost of handling it well. We help you build the policies and processes that make difficult people situations manageable before they become expensive.
